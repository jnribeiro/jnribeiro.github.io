About

I am Civil Engineer with experience as Structural Engineer for 2.5 years at a major metallic construction company – Martifer Constructions. There I had the opportunity to conceive and design the steel works of a number of projects, namely the Morocco Mall Skylights in Casablanca, and the temporary works for the SSE Arena (Hydro arena) in Glasgow. I've spent over 5 years working as a full-time researcher at the University of Coimbra while developing advanced non-linear finite element analysis. In that time I have completed a second Masters (in Steel and Composite construction), and have had the opportunity to develop my skills working with complex FEM models (Abaqus) and programming languages (VBA, python). I’ve attended the fourth edition of the Portuguese BIM course, in which I had the chance to learn about the new state of art in regulations and interoperability, for which final work we’ve received an honourable mention @ the PT BIM Excellence Award2017 (Link to award video). Since June 2017 I’ve joined William Hare at their newly open office in Oporto performing design of steel connections and temporary structures.
